### CHANNELS IDENTIFIED

**1. Hacker News "Who is Hiring" / "Who Wants to be Hired"**
- **Frequency:** Monthly threads (1st of each month)
- **Audience:** High-quality tech talent + startups
- **Pattern:** Direct contact info in posts, minimal filtering
- **Swarm Applicability:** HIGH — direct access to hiring managers

**2. IndieHackers "Looking for Help"**  
- **Status:** Requires JS rendering (SPA), not scrapable via curl
- **Audience:** Indie hackers, solo founders, bootstrappers
- **Sweet Spot:** $2-10k projects (infrastructure setup, CI/CD)
- **Swarm Applicability:** HIGH — our exact target demographic

**3. Show HN (Product Launches)**
- **Pattern:** Founders post MVPs, need infrastructure help
- **Timing:** Hit them right after launch (they're scaling)
- **Swarm Applicability:** MEDIUM — requires timing
